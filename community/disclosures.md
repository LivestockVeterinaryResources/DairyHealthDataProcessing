Many of our maintainers and decision-makers are professionally active in cattle
health and have legitimate commercial relationships. **This is welcome and
normal.** Credibility is protected by transparency, not by exclusion: we
**disclose** relevant interests and **recuse** from decisions where we have a
direct financial stake (see [governance.md](governance.md)).

This register lists relevant financial and organizational interests of
maintainers and Steering Committee members. Disclosed conflicts are acceptable;
undisclosed ones are not.

## How to use this file

- Each maintainer / committee member maintains their own entry.
- Disclose employers, owned companies, and material consulting/contract
  relationships **relevant to cattle health data, products, or services.**
- Update via pull request when circumstances change.
- Recuse from decisions in which you, your employer, or a client has a direct
  financial stake, and note the recusal in the relevant issue/PR.
